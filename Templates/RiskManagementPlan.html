Our Potential Risks:

  • Team Members missing class
  • Team Communication Issues
  • Technical Challenges such as difficulty implementing features or software bugs
  • Project Scope Creep
  • Missed Deadlines
  • Quality Control Issues where the deliverables do not meet the expected quality

Risk Assessment Table

Potential Risks Probability Impact Stakeholder Tolerance Mitigation
Team Members missing class Medium Medium High If a member is unable to attend class, have them be available in Teams chat.
Team Communication Issues Medium High Medium Be active in Teams chat so members can be easily contacted.
Technical Challenges High High Medium Work together and use resources to solve issues.
Project Scope Creep High High Medium Clearly define the scope and require PM approval for additions.
Missed Deadlines Medium High Low Set clear deadlines in the planner and Teams chat.
Quality Control Issues Low Medium Low Set clear expectations on the quality of work.

Roles and Responsibilities:

  • Project Manager:
    • Oversee the risk management process
    • Ensure the scope is properly managed
    • Ensure that deadlines are met
  • Researchers:
    • Identify risks related to data quality
    • Identify risks with research timelines
    • Communicate challenges or potential delays immediately
  • Designers:
    • Identify risks with the User Interface
    • Ensure the design meets the expected quality
    • Identify risks with design execution
  • Developers:
    • Identify software risks and bugs
    • Communicate technical challenges
    • Resolve implementation and technical issues

Risk Management Methodology:

  1. Identify: Identify the possible risks in key areas.
  2. Assess: Assess each risk based on likelihood and impact.
  3. Mitigate: Create strategies to avoid or reduce the impact of risks.
  4. Monitor: Continuously update and reevaluate risks as needed.
